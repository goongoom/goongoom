# Contact Us

Have questions, feedback, or need support? We'd love to hear from you.

## Get in Touch

You can reach us through the following channels:

### Email

For general inquiries, support requests, or feedback, please email us at:

**support@goongoom.com**

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

### Report Abuse

If you need to report abusive content or behavior, please email:

**abuse@goongoom.com**

Please include as much detail as possible, including usernames and screenshots if available.

### Legal Inquiries

For legal matters, including law enforcement requests and DMCA takedown notices:

**legal@goongoom.com**

### Press & Media

For press inquiries and media requests:

**press@goongoom.com**

## Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find your answer in our common questions:

**How do I delete my account?**
You can delete your account from the Settings page. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

**How do I report inappropriate content?**
Use the report button on any question or answer, or email us at abuse@goongoom.com.

**Can I change my username?**
Yes, you can change your username in the Settings page.

**How does anonymous questioning work?**
When you send an anonymous question, your identity is not revealed to the recipient. However, we retain this information for safety and legal compliance purposes.
